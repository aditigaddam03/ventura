Competition Process

Five steps from idea to investor-ready.

Ventura is designed to take you from a rough concept to a polished, professional pitch — with expert guidance every step of the way.

01

Register & Submit

Form your team, choose a name, and submit your base startup idea. You can refine it later — just complete registration before the May 25 deadline to secure your spot.

02

Attend Workshops

Join live workshops throughout June led by industry professionals. Refine your idea, receive expert feedback, and develop your skills as a founder in a hands-on environment.

03

Build Your Pitch

Create a 3–5 minute video showcasing your startup using the Ventura Framework. Prepare your pitch deck and submit all final materials before the July 1 deadline.

04

Face the Judges

Industry professionals evaluate every submission on innovation, market fit, feasibility, and overall impact. Only the strongest and most original ideas advance to the finals.

05

Winners Take Off

Top teams receive prize funding, professional mentorship, and ongoing connections with industry leaders to help launch their venture into the real world.

FAQ

Your questions, answered.

Everything you need to know before you register for Ventura 2026.

Who is eligible to compete?

Any high school student in grades 9–12 from any country in the world is welcome to register and compete. Ventura is open globally and completely free to enter.

How many students can be on a team?

Teams of 2–5 members are strongly encouraged. Solo participants may also register, but are encouraged to collaborate with classmates or peers from their school or community.

What is the Ventura Framework?

The Ventura Framework is a structured approach to building a startup pitch that covers the problem, proposed solution, target market, business model, and real-world impact. Full details and materials are shared during the June workshop sessions.

What do winning teams receive?

Top teams receive prize funding, professional mentorship from industry leaders, and ongoing connections to help launch their venture beyond the competition stage.

Is registration free?

Yes. Registration is completely free and open to all eligible high school students worldwide. There are no entry fees at any stage of the competition.
